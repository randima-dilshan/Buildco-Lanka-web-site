BStud Shearstuds

Overview

Discover the pinnacle of connection technology with BSTUD Shearstuds, engineered for optimal performance in critical structural applications. Our shearstuds are designed to ensure superior load transfer between concrete slabs and structural steel components, enhancing the overall integrity and safety of your construction projects. Available in various sizes to meet diverse structural demands, BSTUD Shearstuds are the go-to choice for professionals seeking reliability and strength. With precision manufacturing and rigorous quality control, these shearstuds offer exceptional durability and performance, making them an indispensable part of modern construction.

Specifications:

  • Available Sizes:
    • 16x75mm
    • 16x100mm
    • 19x100mm
  • Material: High-quality steel designed for strength and durability.
  • Features:
    • Optimal load transfer between concrete and steel.
    • Enhances structural integrity and safety.
    • Precision engineered for reliable performance.
    • Suitable for a wide range of construction applications.
  • Applications:Ideal for use in composite construction, including bridges, high-rises, and industrial buildings, where secure and durable connections are essential.
  • Quality Assurance:Manufactured under strict quality control standards to ensure the highest level of performance and reliability.

BStud Shearstuds are your solution for achieving seamless integration and enhanced structural performance, ensuring your projects stand the test of time with unwavering stability and safety.
