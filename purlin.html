BuildcoTech Hot Dip Galvanized Cee and Zed Purlin

Overview

Introducing the BuildcoTech Hot Dip Galvanized Cee and Zed Purlin – the cornerstone of steel construction. Engineered for excellence, these purlins offer unparalleled strength, durability, and flexibility, serving as the robust foundation your project demands. Crafted from premium-grade steel and designed to meet diverse structural needs, our purlins stand as a testament to enduring quality.

Specifications:

  • Material Grade: High-grade steel (350 Mpa to 450 Mpa)
  • Thickness Options: 1.6mm to 2.4mm
  • Web Heights Available: 75mm, 100mm, 150mm, 200mm, 250mm
  • Zinc Coating Standard: ≥300 g/m²
  • Zinc Coating Upgrade Option: Up to 600 g/m² (upon request)
  • Customization:
    • Tailored lengths
    • Pre-punched holes
  • Surface Finish Options:
    • Regular or Minimized Spangle
    • Dry and Chromated Treatments
    • Bright Finish
