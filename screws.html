Buildex Self Drilling Screws and ITW Australia Products

Overview

ITW Buildex, a division of Illinois Tool Works, is renowned for its innovative and reliable fastening products, notably its self-drilling screws. These screws are engineered to simplify construction tasks by eliminating the need for pre-drilling, offering a fast and efficient way to secure materials.

Innovative Features of ITW Buildex Fastening Solutions

ITW Buildex continues to set industry standards with its comprehensive range of fastening products, each designed with unique features to meet the diverse needs of the construction sector:

  • HiGrip® Technology:Enhances the screw's ability to seal and greatly reduce water entry, ensuring a tighter, more secure fit.
  • EPDM Non-Conductive Sealing Washers: Provide an added layer of protection against electrical conductivity, while also aiding in the prevention of water penetration.
  • ShankGuard® Feature:A specialized design that significantly reduces the risk of shank corrosion, extending the lifespan of the screw in harsh environments.
  • Climaseal® Anti-Corrosion Coatings:Offer superior resistance to corrosion, ensuring the durability and longevity of the screw in various weather conditions.
  • RoofZips® Dual Purpose Screws:Engineered for versatility, these screws are ideal for both metal and timber roofing applications, providing a secure and durable fastening solution.
  • HeadLok Security Screws:Designed for maximum security, these screws feature a unique head that provides increased resistance against tampering and theft.
  • FibreZips Screws:Specially formulated for fixing cement sheeting and compressed boards, offering high performance and reliability in cementitious applications.
  • Specialized Screws for the Steel Frame Industry:Tailored to meet the specific requirements of steel framing, these screws ensure a strong and secure connection in metal construction.
  • Poly Teks & PolyZips for Fixing Clear Roofing:Ideal for transparent roofing materials, these screws are designed to securely fix clear roofing panels without compromising their integrity or appearance.

Each of these features demonstrates ITW Buildex's commitment to innovation, quality, and performance, ensuring that construction professionals have access to the best fastening solutions on the market.

Benefits:
