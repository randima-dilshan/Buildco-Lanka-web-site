What is Buildco Lanka Pvt Ltd

Buildco Lanka (Pvt) Ltd stands as a leading provider of professional steel building solutions in Sri Lanka, offering an extensive array of superior steel building accessories. Our product range encompasses the cutting-edge BuiDeck-R30 roofing and accessories, Hot Dip Galvanized Structural Decking Systems, and Cee and Zed Purlins and Angles, alongside top-tier rainwater accessories. Our commitment to excellence is unwavering, with a focus on quality, sustainability, and forging lasting relationships with our customers.

We are at the forefront of introducing ZAMp products, specially crafted for roofing and purlins in highly corrosive environments, making them ideal for coastal areas, livestock, and agricultural projects. This innovation underscores our dedication to durability and excellence in challenging environments.

Buildco Lanka is privileged to partner with some of Sri Lanka's most esteemed contractors and companies, solidifying our role in the project market. For comprehensive technical insights, we invite you to visit BuildcoTech, our specialized technical resource center. As the authorized dealer for ITW Buildex construction products in Sri Lanka, we are your gateway to world-class building solutions.

Explore Buildco Lanka (Pvt) Ltd for state-of-the-art solutions that endure, reflecting our commitment to innovation, quality, and sustainability.
